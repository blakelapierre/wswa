Disconnection Protections

PG*E and SCE may not disconnect any customer on Medical Baseline or Life Support, or who certifies that he or she has a serious illness or condition that could become life threatening if service is disconnected without an in-person visit from a utility representative.

Such visits should take place within 48 hours, or at the time of disconnection.

This requirement has no expiration date.
